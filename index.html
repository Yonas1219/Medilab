Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ section, where we provide answers to some of the most common questions you might have about our platform. We understand that you may have queries about how our services work, security measures, doctor selection, and more. Explore the questions below to find the information you need, and feel free to reach out to us if you have any additional inquiries. Your health and well-being are our priority, and we're here to ensure you have a seamless and informed experience with us.

  • How does the online consultation work?

    Our platform allows you to connect with a qualified doctor through real-time video consultations or secure messaging. You can choose a doctor, book an appointment, and have a virtual consultation from the comfort of your home.

  • Is the platform secure and private?

    Yes, your privacy and security are our top priorities. We use advanced encryption and secure technology to ensure your personal and medical information is protected. Your consultations are confidential and secure.

  • Can I use my insurance for online consultations?

    We are working on partnerships with insurance providers to offer coverage for our services. Currently, we provide affordable pricing options, and you can pay securely using our payment gateway.

  • Can I get prescriptions through the platform?

    Yes, our doctors can issue online prescriptions when appropriate. After a thorough consultation, if a prescription is needed, it will be sent electronically to a pharmacy of your choice.

  • How do I choose a doctor that suits my needs?

    Our platform provides detailed profiles of all our doctors, including their specialties, experience, and patient reviews. You can browse through these profiles and choose a doctor that aligns with your healthcare needs.
